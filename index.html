Global Occult Coalition — Arizona Unit

Roles, applications, intel, rules, and lore for the Arizona detachment. Rooted in GOC structure, adapted for Discord operations.

Mission

Observe, interdict, sanitize. Arizona Unit codifies intel, coordinates operations, and keeps RP grounded and safe under Discord ToS.

Structure & Roles

⚔️ Command

  • @Director-General – founders, supreme authority.
  • @Deputy Director – second-in-command.
  • @Council Liaison – ceremonial “Council of 108” connection.

🛡️ Operations

  • @High Command – senior staff, branch heads.
  • @Commander – leads divisions (Combat, Research, Intel).
  • @Officer – moderators, enforcement.
  • @Agent – trusted field operatives.

📚 Support & Lore

  • @Research Division – lore, SCP/GOC canon keepers.
  • @Tech Division – bot + script maintainers.
  • @Logistics – events, recruitment, internal support.

🌑 Community

  • @Recruit – new members (probation).
  • @Operative – standard members after vetting.
  • @Veteran – long-term, recognized members.
  • @Civilian – guests/supporters outside RP chain.

🕯️ Optional Flavor (RP / events)

  • @Strike Team ST-[#] – specialized task forces.
  • @Consultant – allies from other groups.
  • @Observer – external SCP Foundation / O5 flavor role.

Rules

  1. Follow Discord ToS & Age Requirement — You must be 13+ and follow Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. No exceptions.
  2. Respect Above All — No harassment, slurs, or personal attacks. Keep debates civil. Disrespect = consequences.
  3. Channel Discipline — Post in the right channel. Keep spam, memes, or off-topic content in their zones.
  4. Zero Tolerance Content — 🚫 No NSFW, gore, piracy, or illegal activity. Instant ban for violations.
  5. No Spam or Raids — Don’t mass ping. Don’t flood chat, soundboards, or VC.
  6. Chain of Command — Respect staff and High Command. Appeals go through proper channels.
  7. Roleplay Separation — Stay IC in RP channels. OOC talk belongs in OOC spaces.
  8. Represent the GOC Professionally — No trolling, impersonation, or behavior that damages the group’s image.
  9. Information Security — Do not leak private server info, docs, or member details. Protect your identity and the Coalition.
  10. Zero Strike Policy — Hate speech, threats, doxxing, or raids = immediate removal.
